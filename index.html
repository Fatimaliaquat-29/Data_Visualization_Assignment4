Graph Visualizations

Source: Zachary's Karate Club. A classic social network dataset showing friendships between 34 members of a karate club. This dataset is highly interconnected and non-hierarchical, making it perfect for graph layouts.

Places all 34 members in a large ring. Useful for highlighting the density of connections across the entire club without node overlap.
